Professional Experience


    Performance Improvement / Data Analyst III

    May 2018 – Present

    Companies are turning to data as strategic assets critical to their successes. As a data analyst, I apply data mining and statistics skills to monitor metrics, identify performance gaps and realize productivity improvement. Some of the projects I led include formulating roadmaps to repurpose underutilized tools, consolidating consumables usage, minimizing revenue leakage and driving operation teams to meet production ramp-up milestones. I also assist in tactical operations to recover revenue lost due to production hiccups. Ultimately, my main task is to collaborate with various stakeholders to make sure our objectives are aligned while constraints are respected.

    Business Intelligence / Capacity Planning Analyst II

    Mar 2016 – May 2018

    My role was to develop algorithms to improve model simulation accuracy using business insights and data generated on the production floor to promote informed decision making. I collaborated with cross-functional teams to drive key strategic metrics and implement "lean" concept to reduce new tool purchase. Some of my achievements include helping pricing team to negotiate for higher profit margins, training less experienced colleagues and driving benchmarking across 5 global sites.

    Project Engineer & Cost Analyst I

    Sep 2014 – Feb 2016

    I started as a project engineer dealing with various stakeholders to implement new products transfer and coordinate the upgrades of 3 running factories. As the project scaled, I took on additional responsibilities in budgeting where I collaborated with the corporate finance and procurement teams to build financial models to better manage cash flow. It was during this time that I developed a strong interest in data analysis and modelling.
